Assignment: App Inventor

Class it was assgined: Computer science Engineering

Number of people in group: 2

Name of app: Quiz Me

Things that were used to create app: Mit App Inventor 2

The task for this project was to create easy to use multi challenging app in mit app inventor 2. My partner and I decide to make working app that test/quizzes the user's Intelligence or knowledge of the past and present people that have made an impact or is making an impact on the world. The app is easy to work and also easy to play if you a familiar with the impactors, but if not it will be a bit challenging. The app consists of a picture of the person the user it trying to identify and 4 button to choose from when picking your answer. Each button have a different name on it, one button has the correct name of the other three are the wrong answer. My role in this project was to create the answers to each question and match them with each picture.
